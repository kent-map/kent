## On The Marsh

[On The Marsh](/20c/20c-on-the-marsh)

![](https://iiif.juncture-digital.org/thumbnail?url=https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/9/98/Harold_Gilman_-_Romney_Marsh_-_B1975.4.329_-_Yale_Center_for_British_Art.jpg)

On the seashore stone turns to pebbles, pebbles turn to sand. Sand cannot turn to water, but on this particular day in August, New Romney is soggy enough to prompt the question. The sea lies as flat as the land, as if working its way in by stealth. Professor Carolyn Oulton in the footsteps of Julian Symons.
